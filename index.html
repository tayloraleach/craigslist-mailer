Craigslist Mailer

  1. Configure email settings below.
  2. Go to Craigslist and search for something you want to monitor.
  3. Hit start and wait. Any new listings that are posted will be emailed to you.
For best results

Be specific as possible. Add price ranges for items, year models for vehicles, add flags for ignoring stuffed keywords, and tick bundle duplicates unless you want to receive a million emails. Checks run every 15 minutes or so.
